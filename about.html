The story

Who is
Becky Wexlin?

This shop came from the dream of a girl who just wanted to work from home in a t-shirt, design cool shirts, and build a life supporting the sweatpants revolution.

Becky Wexlin makes funky shirts for nonconformists, quiet weirdos, and people in recovery from blending in. The kind of people who like their clothes to have a little personality — fun, offbeat, and way more interesting than the usual stuff.

The goal is pretty simple: make cool things, sell cool things, and get a little farther away from office life and a little closer to doing this for real. Less "circling back." More making shirts people actually want to wear.

Thanks for being here and supporting a small business built on strange ideas, solid instincts, and the hope of making a living without becoming unbearably boring.

Frequently asked

Where are the shirts made?

Every order is printed fresh and routed to the production facility closest to your shipping address — which keeps transit time short and shipping emissions low. Production typically takes 2–4 business days before your order ships.

How long does shipping take?

All orders ship within 7 days of purchase. US orders typically arrive in 3–7 days after shipping. International orders may take 7–14 days. You'll receive a tracking number as soon as your order is on its way.

What's the return policy?

Because every piece is printed to order specifically for you, all sales are final and nonrefundable. We strongly recommend checking the size guide before ordering. If your order arrives damaged or there's a production error, we'll replace it immediately — just contact us with a photo.

Can I suggest a design?

Yes and we love it. Send your cursed idea to hello@beckywexlincreative.com or use the contact form. No guarantees but we read everything.

What does "Becky" mean on the tag?

It's the signature. Every Becky Wexlin Creative piece carries the name as a mark of intentionality — a reminder that a real human with a real sense of humour made this thing.
