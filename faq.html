Ordering

How do I place an order?+

Browse the shop, select the item you want, and you'll be taken to the product page where you can choose your size and complete the purchase. All orders are processed securely.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as PayPal and other payment methods available at checkout. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

I placed an order — what happens next?+

You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately after purchase. Your shirt is then sent to production — printed fresh to order at the facility closest to your address. Once it ships, you'll receive a second email with your tracking number. Total time from order to delivery is typically within 7 days.

Can I cancel or change my order after placing it?+

Because production begins quickly after an order is placed, we have a very short window for cancellations or changes — typically within 2 hours of purchase. If you need to cancel or make a change, contact us immediately with your order number and we'll do our best to help. After production has started, changes are no longer possible.

Can I order in bulk?+

Yes — if you're interested in a bulk order for an event, group, or business, get in touch and we'll work something out. Minimum quantities and lead times vary by design.

Shipping & delivery

How long does shipping take?+

All orders ship within 7 days of purchase. Once shipped:

  • — US orders: typically 3–7 days after shipping
  • — International orders: typically 7–14 days after shipping

You'll receive a tracking number by email as soon as your order is on its way.

Do you ship internationally?+

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and estimated delivery windows for your specific country are calculated at checkout. International orders may be subject to customs duties or import taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Do you offer free shipping?+

Yes — free shipping is available on all US orders over $50. Shipping costs for orders under $50 and all international orders are calculated at checkout based on destination.

How do I track my order?+

You'll receive a tracking number by email as soon as your order ships. If you haven't received a tracking email within 7 days of purchase, check your spam folder first, then contact us with your order number and we'll look into it.

My order hasn't arrived — what should I do?+

If your tracking shows the order as delivered but you haven't received it, check with neighbours and your building's mail area first. If it's genuinely missing, contact us with your order number and we'll investigate and sort it out.

Why do you ship from the facility closest to me?+

Each order is routed to the production facility geographically closest to your shipping address. This keeps transit times short, reduces unnecessary shipping miles and associated emissions, and means your shirt arrives faster than if it were shipped from a single central location. It's a better model for the planet and for the customer.

Returns & exchanges

What is your returns policy?+

Because every Becky Wexlin Creative piece is printed to order specifically for you, all sales are final and nonrefundable. We strongly recommend checking the size guide on each product page before placing your order.

If your order arrives damaged or there's a production error, we'll replace it immediately at no cost — just contact us with a photo and your order number.

Can I exchange for a different size?+

We're unable to process size exchanges because every item is printed specifically for the original order. The best way to avoid a sizing issue is to use the size guide on each product page — it includes chest and length measurements for every size. When in doubt, size up; a slightly relaxed fit is always easier to wear than one that's too small.

My shirt arrived damaged or with a defect — what do I do?+

We're sorry about that — it shouldn't happen. Contact us within 14 days of delivery with a clear photo of the damage or defect and your order number. We'll send a replacement as quickly as possible at no charge to you. No arguing, no runaround.

I received the wrong item — what now?+

Contact us with your order number and a photo of what you received. We'll send the correct item immediately. This is our error and we'll fix it fast.

Sizing

What sizes do your shirts come in?+

All Becky Wexlin Creative shirts are available in unisex sizes from XS through 5XL. Every product page includes a size guide with exact chest and length measurements for each size.

How do I choose the right size?+

Measure your chest at the widest point and compare to the size guide on the product page. Our shirts are cut as unisex, meaning they have a relaxed, inclusive fit. If you're between two sizes, we recommend going up — a slightly larger graphic tee sits and drapes better than one that's a little too tight, and the print will thank you over time.

Will my shirt shrink after washing?+

Our shirts are made from pre-shrunk cotton, so you should see minimal shrinkage when washed in cold water. Washing in hot water or tumble drying on high heat can cause shrinkage in any cotton garment — both of which we recommend avoiding anyway to protect the print. Stick to cold wash and air dry and the fit will stay consistent. See our full care guide for details.

Are your shirts true to size?+

Our shirts fit true to a standard unisex sizing. They are cut with a relaxed body — not boxy, not fitted. If you prefer a more fitted look, size down. If you prefer an oversized fit, size up. The measurements on each product page are the most reliable guide.

About our shirts

What fabric are your shirts made from?+

Becky Wexlin Creative shirts are made from high-quality ring-spun or combed ring-spun cotton at a substantial fabric weight designed for durability and print quality. Ring-spun cotton is softer, stronger, and more durable than standard cotton because the fibres are more tightly aligned during the spinning process. Full fabric specifications are listed on each product page.

Want to understand more about what makes a quality graphic tee? We wrote a whole guide: What actually makes a quality graphic tee?

What print method do you use?+

We use direct-to-garment (DTG) printing and screen printing depending on the design. DTG is ideal for complex, multi-colour, and photographic designs — the ink is absorbed directly into the fabric fibres for a smooth, durable finish. Screen printing produces exceptionally vibrant results on simpler designs. The print method for each design is noted on its product page. Both methods produce long-lasting prints when cared for correctly.

Are the colours on the shirts accurate to the product photos?+

We process our product images to represent the actual print as accurately as possible. Minor variations can occur between screens due to differences in monitor calibration and individual screen settings — this is unavoidable in online retail. Product descriptions include notes on primary colours where colour accuracy is particularly important.

Do you offer custom or personalised shirts?+

We don't currently offer custom or personalised shirts. All designs are created by Becky Wexlin Creative. If you have a design concept you'd love to see made, send it to hello@beckywexlincreative.com — we read everything, and good ideas occasionally become real products.

How often do you release new designs?+

New designs drop regularly — we don't announce schedules because part of the fun is the surprise. The best way to stay across new releases is to check the shop's new arrivals section or follow us on social media. Some designs are limited and won't be restocked once they're gone.

Care & washing

How do I wash my graphic tee without ruining the print?+

Four rules that make the biggest difference:

  1. 1. Turn inside out before washing
  2. 2. Wash in cold water (30°C / 86°F or below)
  3. 3. Air dry instead of tumble drying
  4. 4. Use a gentle detergent — skip the fabric softener

For the full breakdown on why each of these matters, read our complete care guide.

Can I tumble dry my shirt?+

We recommend air drying. Heat is the main enemy of printed fabric — tumble dryers run hot enough to damage print quality over time, and the mechanical action adds friction on top. If you must use a dryer, use the lowest heat setting and remove the shirt while still slightly damp to finish air drying.

Can I iron my graphic tee?+

Never iron directly on the print — the heat will damage it. If you need to remove creases, turn the shirt inside out and iron on a low setting, avoiding any areas where the print shows through the fabric. Better yet, air dry flat and the shirt usually comes out wrinkle-free without ironing at all.

Why is the print on my shirt starting to crack?+

Minor cracking on a screen-printed design is normal wear over time — it indicates the top layers of ink are beginning to break down. Switching to cold wash and air drying at this point will slow further degradation significantly. Heat and friction are the main culprits: hot water, tumble dryers, and washing printed shirts inside-out all play a role. Our care guide covers exactly how to prevent this from the start.

About the brand

Who is Becky Wexlin?+

Becky Wexlin Creative is an independent apparel brand making funky, meme-forward, and esoteric clothing for people who refuse to be boring. "Becky" appears on the tag of every shirt as a signature — a mark that something was made with genuine intention and a real sense of humour behind it. Read the full story here.

Why do you make shirts to order rather than keeping stock?+

Printing to order means nothing goes to waste. No unsold inventory, no overproduction, no boxes of shirts nobody wanted sitting in a warehouse. It's a better model for the environment and it means we can release new designs freely without the financial pressure of traditional wholesale. Every shirt is made specifically for the person who ordered it — which we think is a better way to do things.
