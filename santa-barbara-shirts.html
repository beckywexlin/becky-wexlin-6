The backstory

Locally designed Santa Barbara apparel that isn’t from a gift shop

Becky Wexlin Creative is a small apparel brand based in Santa Barbara, California. Every design starts here — between the mountains and the ocean, in a town that’s equal parts beautiful and weird.

We make Santa Barbara t-shirts and Goleta shirts for the people who know what the 805 actually feels like. Not the postcard version. The version where you explain to out-of-towners that In-N-Out is on the wrong side of the freeway and that “downtown Goleta” is a generous term.

Premium blanks. Printed to order. Priced so you can actually afford to be different.
