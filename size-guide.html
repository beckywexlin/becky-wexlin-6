Step one

How to measure yourself

All you need is a soft tape measure and 60 seconds.

1

Chest

Wrap the tape measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping it level and parallel to the floor. Keep it snug but not tight. This is your chest circumference.

2

Compare to the chart

Our size charts show half-chest measurements (the width of the shirt laid flat). To compare, divide your chest measurement by 2. Or just match your full chest measurement to the "chest circumference" column.

3

When in doubt

If you're between two sizes, go up. A slightly relaxed fit always looks more intentional than a tee that's a little too tight — and the print quality holds better with less tension on the fabric.

Not sure which to pick?

Gildan 5000 vs Bella+Canvas 3001

Two great shirts, two different personalities. Here's how to pick.

Choose Gildan 5000 if...

  • You want a durable, heavyweight shirt that lasts for years
  • You prefer a relaxed, classic fit with room to move
  • You're buying for everyday wear and want something that can take a beating
  • You wear larger sizes — it goes up to 5XL
  • You want the most print-friendly surface for bold, graphic designs

Choose Bella+Canvas 3001 if...

  • You want the softest possible shirt against your skin
  • You prefer a slimmer, more modern retail fit
  • You care about the tear-away label detail
  • You want a lighter shirt for warmer weather
  • You're buying as a gift and want something that feels premium straight out of the box
