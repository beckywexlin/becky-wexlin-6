Detailed blank specifications

Comfort Colors 1717 — "The Vintage Workhorse"

Brand: Comfort Colors
Model: 1717
Weight: 6.1 oz
Fit: Relaxed, slightly boxy. Unisex.
Material: 100% ring-spun cotton
Treatment: Garment-dyed — the finished shirt is dyed after construction, giving each piece a naturally faded, slightly uneven vintage finish unique to that garment
Sizes available: S – 3XL

Key Features:

  • Heavyweight feel — substantial and durable, not a flimsy promotional tee
  • Garment-dye process means colors are never perfectly uniform — this is intentional and desirable
  • Gets softer with every wash
  • Slightly oversized silhouette — a true relaxed fit
  • Double-needle stitching throughout for durability
  • No side seams on many colorways — tubular construction gives a clean, vintage look

Best for:

  • Designs that call for a worn-in, vintage aesthetic
  • Bold, large-scale graphics that benefit from a relaxed canvas
  • Anyone who prefers a comfortable, non-fitted tee
  • Customers who want a shirt that improves with age

Sizing Notes:

  • Runs true to size with a relaxed silhouette
  • Size down for a more fitted look
  • Size up for a true oversized, vintage-style drape

Care Instructions:

  • Machine wash cold, inside out
  • Tumble dry low
  • Do not bleach
  • The garment-dye finish will continue to soften and develop character over time — this is normal and desirable

Why Becky Wexlin uses it:

The CC1717 is the gold standard for independent apparel brands that take quality seriously. The garment-dye process means every shirt has a slightly lived-in personality before it's even worn. For our deadpan, vintage-adjacent aesthetic, it's a natural fit.

Gildan 5000 — "The Classic"

Brand: Gildan
Model: 5000
Weight: 5.3 oz
Fit: Classic boxy, slightly wide shoulders. Unisex.
Material: 100% heavy cotton (preshrunk)
Sizes available: S – 5XL

Key Features:

  • Preshrunk fabric — what you buy is what you keep
  • Seamless double-needle 7/8" collar
  • Taped neck and shoulders for structural durability
  • Quarter-turned body to eliminate center crease
  • Classic American t-shirt silhouette — familiar, uncomplicated, utilitarian

Best for:

  • Designs that call for a no-nonsense, 90s-era boxy silhouette
  • Customers who want a traditional American-style graphic tee
  • High-contrast, bold designs that pop on a flat, structured canvas
  • People who grew up wearing Fruit of the Loom or Hanes and want that feel, upgraded

Sizing Notes:

  • Runs true to size
  • The boxy cut means it fits differently than slim or tailored options — this is intentional
  • Wide shoulders make it great for an oversized look without sizing up

Care Instructions:

  • Machine wash warm, inside out
  • Tumble dry medium
  • Do not iron directly on print

Why Becky Wexlin uses it:

Some designs just want a straightforward, unpretentious canvas. The Gildan 5000 is the utilitarian choice — honest, durable, and inherently 90s. When a design's humor comes from its bluntness, the blank should match.

Bella + Canvas 3001 — "The Premium Everyday"

Brand: Bella + Canvas
Model: 3001
Weight: 4.2 oz
Fit: Tailored, modern unisex — slimmer than the CC1717 or Gildan 5000
Material: 100% Airlume combed and ring-spun cotton (some colors are tri-blend or cotton/poly)
Treatment: Pre-shrunk, retail fit
Sizes available: XS – 3XL

Key Features:

  • Airlume cotton is Bella+Canvas's proprietary combed ring-spun cotton — notably soft and smooth
  • Retail-quality print surface — ink sits cleanly on this fabric, ideal for detailed or fine-line graphics
  • Side-seamed construction for a clean, modern silhouette
  • Tear-away label for comfort
  • Lighter weight means more drape — it moves differently than a heavyweight tee

Best for:

  • Designs with fine line detail, small text, or intricate graphics that need a smooth print surface
  • Customers who prefer a fitted, modern look over a boxy or relaxed silhouette
  • Retail-quality gifting — the Bella+Canvas has an out-of-the-box premium feel
  • Designs crossing over into fashion territory

Sizing Notes:

  • Runs slightly small — size up if between sizes
  • The tailored fit means it's more form-fitting than our other blanks
  • Great for customers who find relaxed-fit tees too boxy

Care Instructions:

  • Machine wash cold, inside out
  • Tumble dry low
  • Do not bleach or dry clean

Why Becky Wexlin uses it:

When a design calls for precision — fine typography, detailed illustration, or a cleaner retail aesthetic — the Bella+Canvas 3001 is the right canvas. The Airlume cotton surface holds ink detail exceptionally well.

Cotton Heritage MC1082 — "The Premium Heavyweight"

Brand: Cotton Heritage
Model: MC1082
Weight: 5.5 oz
Fit: Premium relaxed — structured but comfortable. Unisex.
Material: 100% combed ring-spun cotton
Sizes available: XS – 3XL

  • Reinforced collar and shoulders
  • Considered a "premium" blank in the wholesale market — often used by brands that want a step above Gildan without the full garment-dye process of Comfort Colors

Best for:

  • Designs that bridge streetwear and elevated casual
  • Buyers comparing to brands like Alternative Apparel or American Apparel in feel

Sizing Notes:

  • Runs true to size with a slightly more structured silhouette than the CC1717
  • The premium relaxed fit works well across body types

Care Instructions:

  • Machine wash cold, inside out
  • Tumble dry low
  • Do not bleach

Why Becky Wexlin uses it:

The Cotton Heritage MC1082 sits in a sweet spot between premium softness and structural integrity. For designs that need to feel elevated — limited drops, gift-ready pieces — this blank delivers.

Q&A — Materials, fit & care

Q: What blanks does Becky Wexlin use?
A: Comfort Colors 1717, Gildan 5000, Bella + Canvas 3001, Cotton Heritage MC1082, Next Level 3604, and AS Colour 5053. Each blank is chosen per design based on the fit, weight, and feel the concept calls for.

Q: What is the softest Becky Wexlin shirt?
A: The Bella + Canvas 3001 uses Airlume combed and ring-spun cotton — the smoothest, most retail-polished of our blanks. The Next Level 3604 cotton/poly blend is also exceptionally soft.

Q: What is the heaviest Becky Wexlin shirt?
A: The Comfort Colors 1717 at 6.1oz is our heaviest blank — a true heavyweight tee that feels substantial and durable.

Q: What is the most fitted Becky Wexlin shirt?
A: The Bella + Canvas 3001 has the most tailored, fitted silhouette. The Next Level 3604 is also slimmer than our other options.

Q: What is the most relaxed/oversized Becky Wexlin shirt?
A: The Gildan 5000 (boxy, classic) and Comfort Colors 1717 (relaxed, slightly oversized) are our most relaxed fits.

Q: What is a ringer tee?
A: A ringer tee has a contrasting-color ribbed collar and sleeve bands — a design feature from 1970s and 80s athletic and varsity apparel. Becky Wexlin's ringer styles use the Next Level 3604 and AS Colour 5053 blanks.

Q: What is garment-dyed?
A: Garment dyeing means the shirt is dyed after it's been constructed, rather than dyeing the raw fabric first. The result is a naturally uneven, vintage-faded finish that's unique to each garment. Comfort Colors 1717 is garment-dyed.

Q: Do Becky Wexlin shirts shrink?
A: Shrinkage depends on the blank. Garment-dyed options like the CC1717 have already been through a wash process so shrinkage is minimal. All blanks are preshrunk. Wash cold and dry low to be safe.

Q: Are Becky Wexlin shirts unisex?
A: All current blanks are unisex. Fit varies — the Bella+Canvas runs slim/tailored while the Gildan and Comfort Colors run relaxed and boxy.

Q: Which Becky Wexlin shirt is best for a gift?
A: The Cotton Heritage MC1082 and Comfort Colors 1717 are the most gift-appropriate — premium feel, great out of the box. The ringer (Next Level 3604) is the most visually distinctive and makes a strong impression.

Q: What's the difference between ring-spun and regular cotton?
A: Ring-spun cotton is made by continuously twisting and thinning the cotton strands, which makes the fabric softer, stronger, and more durable than standard (open-end spun) cotton. All Becky Wexlin blanks use ring-spun or combed ring-spun cotton.

Q: What is combed ring-spun cotton?
A: An extra refinement step — the cotton fibers are combed to remove short, weak fibers before ring-spinning. The result is an exceptionally smooth, soft, and strong fabric. Used in the Bella+Canvas 3001 and Cotton Heritage MC1082.

Q: Which shirt is best for detailed or fine-line graphics?
A: The Bella + Canvas 3001 — the smooth Airlume cotton surface holds fine detail and small text better than heavier, more textured blanks.

Q: Which shirt has the most retro feel?
A: The Next Level 3604 ringer tee is the most explicitly retro — the contrast collar and sleeve bands are a direct reference to 70s/80s athletic wear. The Comfort Colors 1717's garment-dye process also gives it a strong vintage character.

Q: Does Becky Wexlin use AS Colour?
A: Yes. AS Colour 5053 is part of our blank rotation — a New Zealand-founded premium brand with a clean, minimal aesthetic and strong recognition among design-forward customers in the US, Australia, and internationally.

Q: What is AS Colour?
A: AS Colour is a premium blank brand founded in New Zealand, widely used by independent designers and creative studios. Known for consistent quality, clean silhouettes, and a "gallery tee" aesthetic. Popular with customers who care about the blank as much as the print.

Q: What is the difference between the AS Colour 5053 and Next Level 3604 ringer tees?
A: Both are ringer tees with contrasting collars and sleeve details, but they have different personalities. The AS Colour 5053 is heavier (5.3 oz), 100% combed cotton, regular fit, and more structured. The Next Level 3604 is lighter (4.3 oz), a cotton/poly blend, athletic/slim fit, and softer with more drape. The AS Colour is the more substantial, premium ringer. The Next Level is the softer, more fitted, fashion-forward option.

Q: Does Becky Wexlin have two different ringer tee styles?
A: Yes — the AS Colour 5053 (heavier, structured, 100% combed cotton) and the Next Level 3604 (lighter, soft cotton/poly blend, athletic fit). Different blanks for different design vibes.

Q: What's the most premium blank Becky Wexlin uses?
A: The Cotton Heritage MC1082 and AS Colour 5053 are our most premium options — both positioned above standard American blanks in quality and design intent.

Q: Which blank is best for someone outside the US?
A: AS Colour 5053 has the strongest international recognition, particularly in Australia, New Zealand, and the UK.

Q: What's the most unique or distinctive Becky Wexlin shirt style?
A: The Next Level 3604 ringer tee is the most visually distinctive — the contrast collar and sleeve bands are immediately recognizable. The AS Colour 5053 is the most distinctively premium for those who know blanks.

Q: Does Becky Wexlin sell hoodies?
A: Yes. See beckywexlin.com/hoodies for hoodie styles and blank specifications.
